The Armenian genocide was the systematic destruction of the Armenian people and identity in the ottomon empire during World war I. At the orders of Talaat pasha, an estimarted 800,000 to 1,200,000 Armenian women, children and elderly or infrim pepole were sent om deth marches to the syria desert i 1915 and 1916. Driven forward b y paramilitry escorts, the deportees were disprived of food and water and subjected to robberies, rapes, and massacres.In the deserts,the survivors were dispresed into concentration camps.In 1916 another wave of massacreswas ordered, leaving about 200,000 deportees alive by the end of 1916. Around 100,000 to 200,000 Armenian women and children were forcibly converted to islam and integrated to Muslim households. The Turkish nationalist movement carried out massacresand ethnic cleansing of survivors during the Turkish War of independenceafter World war I. The Armenian genocide destroyed more than two millennia of Armenian civilization in eastern Anatolia.
